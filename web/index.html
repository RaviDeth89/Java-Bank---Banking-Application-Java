About

"JAVA BANK" is a banking web-application created by M.Sc.IT Students. This web-application is made up using source files including HTML, CSS, Java, Filters, JSP as well as JSTL for easy customization.

"JAVA BANK" provides you all banking facilities like Net-Banking and ATM. You can create your own bank account on this website. Using that, you can transfer money from your account to another account, login with ATM card and through that you can withdraw cash and also deposit cash to your account. You can fix your deposit amount and get more benefit.
